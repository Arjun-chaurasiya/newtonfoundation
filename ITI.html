Industrial Training Institute (ITI)

Industrial Training Institute (ITI)


ITI-Industrial Training Institute is a Technical course one can join after successful completion of class 10th or Matriculation.
ITI is a great course for students who want to do a professional technical course in the short term.
ITI holders can get a good job in electrical, mechanical, and other manufacturing sectors. So many students are getting Jobs in railways nowadays after ITI.

List of Industrial Training Institute -

Fitter


ITI Fitter is a mechanically related branch, Where you get to learn about fitting.
ITI Fitter syllabus includes the knowledge about fittings like Pipe fittings, Machine fitting, and structure fittings are the main working areas, on which a fitter works.
Within this 2-year course, you are given such knowledge so that you can work with any kind of fitting.
After completing the ITI Fitter course, you have a lot of career options out of which two are the major ones that either you can go for higher education or you can go for a job.

Electrician


ITI Electrician is a 2-year regular course offered by Industrial Training Institute to train the students to work at the industrial level so that you are able to work as an electrician.
ITI Electrician course helps the student to learn in detail about electrical wiring, electric field and provide deep information about it.
This course also provides all the fundamental knowledge about the electrical field to the students who are willing to do this course.
The students are provided knowledge about the various types of electrical equipment such as electric motors, electric fans, refrigerators, washing machines, electric bells, etc.

Physiotherapy


Physiotherapy technician is one of the most popular healthcare course that deals with applying modes of physical movement. Physiotherapy is a course that allows students to work with human anatomy.
Physiotherapy Technician is suitable for government job and private job.
Physiotherapy Technician is very powerful for self-empowerment.
On successful completion of this course, the candidates shall be gainfully employed as:
Assistant Physiotherapist, Healthcare Advisor, Self Employment.

Health Sanitary Inspector


Health Sanitary Inspector trade is one-year duration course, a candidate is trained
on Professional Skill, Professional Knowledge and Employability Skill related to job role. In
addition to this, a candidate is entrusted to undertake project work, extracurricular activities
and on-the-job training to build up confidence.
Health Sanitary Inspector students are taught about the classification of food, a balanced diet, nutrition education malnutrition, therapeutic diet, food preservation, refrigeration, purification of water, and many more terms.
Health Inspector / Sanitary Inspector is a definite post in the government departments at the state and the center level. Government departments include departments like Municipal Corporations, Nagar Palikas, Railways, Hospitals, Cantonment Area, Government Institutes, Oil Refineries, and Factories etc. In the private organizations they work with Hospitals, Hotel, Metal Industry, Chemical Industry, Textile Industry, Food Industry, Travel and Entertainment Industry, Electrical Industry etc.

Computer & Hardware Networking


computer & hardware course is a computer hardware vocation course, this course introduces the candidate to diverse topics and skills.
This includes installing hardware, maintaining and repairing computer systems, troubleshooting, setting up the computer, setting up the computer security system, configuring the computer, offering onsite and offsite technical support, etc.
The primary function of computer hardware is to support functions such as processing, communication, secondary storage and input and output. The main purpose of a computer network is to exchange data and share resources with other computing devices.
