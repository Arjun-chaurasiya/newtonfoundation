Vision & Mission of Newton Foundation
Vision :

- We are providing one-stop solutions for the students and help them achieve their goals through proper career counseling & guidance, to add value for our partner institutions and all other stakeholders.

Mission :

- To provide customized solutions to the students aspiring to study.
To build a strong and credible relationship with the partner institutions by recognizing values & goals.
To match the student’s abilities, performance, and desires with the best possible career options.

Goals:

- To help the students in identifying the proper courses and selecting good institutions to fulfill their desired goals.
To develop the necessary skills and knowledge in the field of career counseling for the key team members.
- To develop the strong team spirit and provide quality solutions to the students.
- To get the feedback from the students in order to improve our services in future and make it better for the future students.
